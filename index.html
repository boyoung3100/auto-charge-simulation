🏭 Auto Charge 2.0 — AMR Factory Simulation

Click an AMR resting at its return point (dashed ring) to dispatch one job: pick up a part at a top station, carry it down to a drop-off station, then head back — one job per trip. No two AMRs share a station. Toggle Auto-run to dispatch continuously instead. The battery check happens when a job finishes — if it's at or below the low-battery limit the AMR diverts straight to the nearest free charger (or a standby spot) instead of going to its return point, charges to the resume limit, and returns. Turn on is_idle to also trigger charging while an AMR sits idle at its return point; with it off, an idle AMR runs flat and powers off.
